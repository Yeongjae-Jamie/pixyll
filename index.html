I'm a prospective Ph.D. student seeking to enter graduate schools in mechanical engineering. My research interest lies in designing robust control systems for robots. Specifically, I'm interested in 1) modeling strategies that make the nonlinear dynamics of robots easy to control, 2) control methodologies that allow robots to execute various tasks, and 3) the development of control systems that enable machines to operate robustly in various environments. I conceive of a world where humans can collaborate with robots in hazardous environments, such as disaster zones, space exploration, and radioactive areas. Consequently, I aim to reduce the risk of certain tasks and broaden the scope of human activity.
